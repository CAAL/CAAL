About

Caal is a web-based tool for modelling and verification of concurrent systems developed at Aalborg University. Concurrent systems are modelled using the CCS language.

It can visualise CCS processes in the explorer and check for strong and weak bisimulation and HML properties in the verifier.

Authors: Jacob K. Wortmann, Jesper Riemer Andersen, Nicklas Andersen, Mathias Munk Hansen, Simon Reedtz Olesen, and SÃ¸ren Enevoldsen.
